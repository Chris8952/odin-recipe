Breaded chicken with a red sauce and parmesan on top

Chicken Parmesan is no doubt the most popular non-pasta dish in Italian-American style restaurants. And while this delicious meal may seem fancy, it's actually pretty easy to make at home. In fact, chicken Parmesan is one of those meals that's often best made in your own kitchen.

  • 3 boneless chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Pound chicken to even thickness
  2. Salt and pepper the chicken
  3. Add parmesan to the bread crumbs
  4. Rest the chicken for 15 minutes
  5. Put a even amount of sauce and parmesan onto the chicken
  6. Heat the oven to 450 degrees farenheit and cook until slightly brown
