Pizza with crispy pepperoni on top

Pizza is a staple in most households to have once a week but homemade pizza is a fun twist to the whole process.

  • Flour
  • pizza sauce
  • Mozzerela cheese
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Put the sauce on the dough
  3. put the cheese over the sauce
  4. put pepperoni over the cheese
  5. Bake the pizza
