Meatloaf with a Ketchup sauce on top

The best meatloaf recipe is one that is easy to make and full of flavor. Ground beef, seasonings, onions, and a sprinke of breadcrumbs with a zesty topping. The 2lb meatloaf starts with 80/20 beef because of how juicy it makes the meatloaf. Then you add in seasoned Breadcrumbs mixed with an egg to help keep the meatloaf together and to hold the perfect texture. Then to give it its delicious taste you add the seasonings which are, onions, parseley, italian seasonings, salt, and pepper. And to top it off you make a zesty ketchup sauce that includes ketchup, chilli sauce, and a little brown sugar. After your done with all that, you then go ahead and set your oven to 350 degrees farenheit and let cook for 55-65 minutes.

  • Ground beef
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Parseley
  • Italian seasonings
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • chilli sauce
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Soften the onion in a pan
  2. Comine all the ingredients in a bowl
  3. Form into a loaf and bake
  4. Top with the meatloaf sauce and bake until bubbly
